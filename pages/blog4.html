Flora: Focusing on Something New

Over spring break, I decided to travel to Sicily in order to get a change of scenery and experience a completely new part of Italian culture. While the architecture and city layout differs greatly from that of Florence, perhaps the biggest difference I noticed was in the plant life. Disclaimer, I don't really know what the flora looks like in Florence considering there is almost no space for it to grow, but I assume it would be quite similar to that of the rest of Tuscany. In Sicily, the plant life was reminiscent of tropical areas I had been to as well as the desert. Massive cacti lined the roads and palm trees cast much needed swaths of shade.
