Patterns: Looking at Italy in Black and White

For this last blog post I decided to focus on an aspect of photography that I find particularly interesting: capturing images in black and white. To me, monochrome photography is one of the best ways to capture the true essense of an image. By removing color, you can heighten the attention to detail. In most of these images, I looked for objects that had some sort of repeating pattern or were effected by light in an interesting way.
